## The question

Is the "5 organs + capstone" the right decomposition for the 0.7.x organ layer (L2, sensing on the stroma)?
Specifically: (a) do organs split into sub-organs? (b) are there organs we haven't named? (c) what's the
build-order? (d) which are 0.7.x vs beyond-horizon (`on_hold`)?

The 5 as chartered: self-non-self · effector-arm · starts-helping · repertoire-scans-you · marks-get-teeth,
plus a capstone (opsonization).

---

## Boyd's destruction — the one error six lenses shared

Six of eight lenses (aristotle, outsider, contrarian, discovery, systems-thinking, expansionist) ran the
same move: map the 5 organs onto ADR-037's six-stage control loop (SENSE→COMPARE→ROUTE→ACT→FEEDBACK→SIZE),
find ROUTE and FEEDBACK "empty," and conclude *re-cut the organ layer into six stage-organs.*

**Boyd rejects it.** The loop-stages are a **failure-class taxonomy for antigen's own machinery** — a sort-key
for *what can break in the regulator* — **not a work-breakdown structure for what to build.** The proof is on
disk: `decisions.md:9808` — ROUTE and FEEDBACK "have **no user-code disturbance-genus** (they are
antigen-response-only stages)." The ratified frame itself marks those two stages as *different material*: they
regulate antigen, they don't sense user code. You cannot cut the build-plan along a seam the frame explicitly
marks as different material. **Organs live *at* loop-coordinates but are individuated by build + beneficiary +
authorization-gate — the stage is where an organ lives, not what makes it one.** ROUTE isn't empty; it's
*correctly thin*. The lenses mistook correctly-thin for missing.

> **The "and", added by Tekgy after the ruling:** the loop-as-taxonomy is right, *and* the six stages are still
> legitimately buildable — not as organs, but as **antigen's own self-regulation / control-plane** (antigen
> instrumenting its own control loop so it catches when *its own* machinery misfires). See "The control-plane
> track" below. Boyd's own rubric already demands the loop be *named*-complete (not silently truncated); this
> makes the non-organ half of that map a first-class build-track.

## The three recognitions (unanimous + substrate-confirmed — ratified, not designed)

   no beneficiary-absorb of its own (`v0.6-capstone-opsonization/launch.md:8` self-declares "a JOIN, not a

2. **self/non-self (0.6.1) is stroma-independent** — it senses on the *signer set*, not the code graph
   (`launch.md:68`). It ships in parallel from day one, off the critical path.
3. **The effector is ONE arm with an internal maturation gradient, cut by *authorization tier*** (own-markers →
   human-SUGGEST → sub-clause-F auto-apply), each behind a stricter gate — not by loop-stage.

## The three rulings (where the lenses genuinely conflicted)

**A · ROUTE — the dread-escalation HOOK is 0.7.x; the full ROUTE-ARM organ is `on_hold` (named).**
aristotle overreached ("forced 6th organ, whole thing in-horizon"); the decomposition under-shipped ("all
on_hold"). The substrate splits it exactly: `route-arm/idea.md` — the dread-escalation hook is *design-now /
near-free* (the Finding schema already carries cluster-key + site), but `cargo antigen review <diff>` is
MODERATE and the full gradient-field lymph-node is gated. **Ship the hook as a schema-reservation + one cheap
`review <diff>` CLI embryo; the full ROUTE-ARM is on_hold, named in the loop-map.**

**B · FEEDBACK — a real organ, but it ships WITH the capstone, not before it.** systems/contrarian/discovery
were right that biology forbids a naked amplifier (never ship the auto-writer before its damper). But their
*sequencing* is wrong by the absorb-law: the forgetting-curve's beneficiary — *developer-attention-being-
drowned* — **does not exist until marks land at scale.** Resolution: **split feedback-homeostasis.** Its
*substrate* (the emit-bus + SCRAM host, "already build-now") co-develops with the stroma write-path, so the
SCRAM rail exists the instant the effector can over-react; its *logic* (forgetting-curve + cascade-governor)
ships **coupled to the capstone**, where its beneficiary is finally real. The amplifier and its damper close
the loop together. **This is the one place Boyd agrees the current plan is genuinely under-built** (the
governor logic was left at charter-tier).

**C · the germinal-center / LEARN "organ" — NOT re-planned.** `antigen::learn` is shipped; the shared context
is authoritative. The expansionist's real catch — "the repertoire scans you but never *matures from having
scanned you*" — is not a new organ; it is the **per-source reputation-update stroma primitive** (confidence
learned-from-being-right, updated on confirmed-vs-dismissed disposition). Both the immunology lens and the
distributed-systems reputation-rhyme converge on this same primitive. **Homed as a sub-slice of the SENSE
organ (0.6.4), not a new organ.** The complement/inverted-default layer needs the deferred reaction-diffusion
field → `on_hold`, named.

## The rubric (Deliverable 1)

**Organ-hood law.** A 0.7.x organ is a capability with (1) a distinct BUILD, (2) a distinct BENEFICIARY-ABSORB
(a different consumer to re-point onto it, per the capability-lifecycle law), and (3) a live STROMA-SIGNAL *or*
a distinct AUTHORIZATION-GATE that exists or ships in the same horizon. **The loop-stage is its coordinate, not
its boundary** — two capabilities can share a stage (the three ACT-tiers); one capability can be denied
organ-hood despite owning a stage (ROUTE, this horizon).

**0.7.x vs `on_hold` — four gates (pass ALL to be in-horizon):**
1. **Stroma-signal ready** — its query (`reachable_from` / `field_at` / `provenance_of` / `blast_from`) is on
   the near side of the closure build, or it needs no query. (Heaviest tier — `blast_from`+SCC-condensation —
   is later than `reachable_from`.)
2. **Beneficiary exists now** — a real shipped consumer to re-point onto it.
3. **Not gated on an unbuilt subsystem** — no runtime arm, distributed registry, or not-yet-existing afferent
   stream.
4. **Substrate flagged build-now, or purely logical on existing substrate.**
Fail any → `on_hold`, but **NAME it in the loop-map** so the loop is *known*-complete.

**Atomic vs split — SPLIT if it fails ANY:** (1) **authorization-gate** (different validation gates → split
into tiers); (2) **stroma-signal** (different query/closure-tier → split by build-order, cheap half first);
(3) **disjoint-absorb** (re-points disjoint consumer-sets → split; the suppression-rethink precedent — it was
literally pulled out of opsonization for this reason); (4) **belongs-iff** (needs an "A *or* B" disjunction →
the *or* is the split-line).

**Build-order:** stroma-closure first (the one unbuilt node four paths converge on) → stroma-independent work
in parallel → SENSE before ACT → damper-*with*-amplifier → JOIN last.

## The control-plane track (the loop, made buildable without being organs)

Boyd's ruling: the loop is a diagnostic taxonomy, not the organ-WBS. Tekgy's "and": the six stages are still a
legitimate **build-track** — antigen's own self-regulation, sensing *antigen's* operation rather than user
code. Named for loop-known-completeness (some already surface in the organ map as substrate/hooks):

| ADR-037 stage | control-plane build-item (non-organ) | status in the map |
|---|---|---|
| SENSE | antigen's own detectors fire correctly (self-scan integrity) | latent — dogfood gate exists |
| COMPARE | antigen's own classification integrity | latent |
| ROUTE | antigen routes its *own* attention (dread-escalation) | **Rh** hook is here (0.7.x) |
| ACT | antigen's own effector integrity / provenance | **2a** provenance is here (0.7.x) |
| FEEDBACK | antigen's own signal legibility (SCRAM/emit-bus) | **Fs** substrate is here (0.7.x) |
| SIZE | antigen tracks its own moving target (adaptation) | `on_hold` (needs runtime-afferent) |

The organ track (senses user code) and the control-plane track (regulates antigen) share substrate at ROUTE
(dread-hook), ACT (provenance), and FEEDBACK (SCRAM) — build those once, both tracks consume. The remaining
control-plane items (SENSE/COMPARE self-integrity, SIZE) are candidate build-outs to scope; they are NOT
prerequisites for the organ layer.

## Addendum — de-risking order ≠ developmental order (the expansionist's quiet finding)

The expansionist lens surfaced a tension Boyd's build-order *follows* but never *names*: the map's order is a
**de-risking order** (stroma-first; prove the effector on its own markers before anything touches external
code — "understand-before-intervene, safe-surface-first"), which is **not** the biological **developmental
order** (central-tolerance → innate/complement → afferent recognition → germinal-center/LEARN → effector-last).
Two places the difference bites, worth naming so the choice is explicit, not smuggled:

- **The effector (2-0) lands before the full SENSE organ (S)** — correct on the de-risking axis (safest
  surface first), backwards on the developmental axis (you sense→learn *before* you act). The project is
  choosing de-risking order; that's defensible, but it should be a stated choice.
- **The germinal-center / LEARN residue.** Boyd demoted it to a primitive (`Sr`, reputation-update). The
  biology insists it is the *only knowledge-generating organ*, and the distributed-systems reputation-rhyme
  independently demands the same primitive — two domains converging on one hole. Ruling stands (primitive, not
  organ, this horizon), but flagged: **if the reputation-update turns out to want its own lifecycle, revisit
  organ-hood at the ceremony.** The convergence of two independent domains on the same missing piece is signal,
  not noise.

(One more the expansionist raised, already honored in the map: the effector's *cytotoxic vs regulatory (Treg)*
lineage split — suppression is `Sup`, a node distinct from the effector tiers, not a mode of the effector.)

## The three decisions for the ratification ceremony

1. **The stage-loop is a diagnostic taxonomy, NOT the build-WBS — reject "re-cut into six stage-organs."**
   Load-bearing; overruling it changes the whole map. (→ candidate ADR-071.) *Amended by Tekgy:* the loop is
   *also* a legitimate non-organ control-plane build-track.
2. **FEEDBACK ships in the same wave as the capstone** — substrate early (with the write-path), logic with the
   JOIN. Corrects the one genuinely-under-built spot (governor logic at charter-tier). (→ organ map / expedition
   sequencing.)
3. **The effector is ONE arm in THREE authorization-tiers; "starts-helping" is tier-1, not a new organ; the
   germinal-center is NOT re-planned** — the residue is the reputation-update stroma primitive (in SENSE).
   (→ folds effector + starts-helping + marks-get-teeth into their honest shapes.)
